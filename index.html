Regulatory Framework

This assessment tool is based on Directive (EU) 2022/2555 (NIS2), transposed into Belgian law by the Law of 7 April 2024 on cybersecurity measures. Evaluation criteria reflect obligations for Important and Essential Entities as defined by the Centre for Cybersecurity Belgium (CCB).

This assessment provides strategic orientation and does not constitute formal legal compliance audit or legal advice.
